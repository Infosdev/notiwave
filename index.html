FAQ

Questions, answered

Will my WhatsApp number get banned?

No. Notiwave uses the official WhatsApp Business API from Meta, not unofficial automation tools. Messages are sent through approved templates, which keeps your number fully compliant.

Do my customers need to install anything?

No. Messages arrive in their normal WhatsApp, just like a message from any business.

Can I send messages in Tamil?

Yes. Templates can be created in Tamil, English or both — approval works the same way.

How is this different from WhatsApp Business app broadcasts?

The free app needs you to send everything manually, and broadcasts only reach people who saved your number. Notiwave sends automatically on schedule, to every customer, with delivery reports.

What happens when the beta ends?

Early access users get a locked-in discount on their first year. You'll never be moved to a paid plan without confirming it first.
